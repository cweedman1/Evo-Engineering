Southwest Systems Integrators Legacy

Roots · Integrity · Continuity

System Legacy

Evo Engineering stands on the foundation of Southwest Systems Integrators: a legacy built through decades of automation, fabrication, and industrial system integration.

Founded by Michael Weedman, SSI became synonymous with precision, reliability, and execution. From large-scale material handling systems to industrial automation projects across major facilities, the work consistently delivered where downtime, handoffs, and field constraints mattered.

Clients Served

  • PepsiCo
  • Coca-Cola
  • Amazon
  • CenterPoint Energy (Houston)
  • Schlumberger
  • Tito's Vodka - first automated bottling line installation
  • Artco-Bell Corporation (Temple, Texas)
  • Textiles, food & beverage, and industrial plants across Texas and beyond

What Carries Forward

SSI was always about integration: making equipment, automation, and operations function as a unified system. Evo carries that forward across manufacturing systems, software, energy, and infrastructure work without sacrificing the reliability that built the name.

Why It Matters

Legacy matters because it preserves operational knowledge. Evo does not treat older systems as debris; it translates useful context into systems that can be operated, audited, and maintained.
