Legacy Systems & Integration

Evo Engineering stands on the shoulders of Southwest Systems Integrators. Our roots run deep in systems automation, fabrication, and integration, carried forward through decades of trust and results.

Founded by Michael Weedman, our family name became synonymous with precision and reliability. From ducting systems for Amazon facilities to material handling and automation projects for major clients, our work has always delivered.

Clients We’ve Served

  • PepsiCo
  • Coca-Cola
  • Amazon
  • CenterPoint Energy (Houston)
  • Schlumberger
  • Tito’s Vodka (we installed their first automated bottling line)
  • Artco-Bell Corporation (Temple, Texas)
  • Textiles, food & beverage, and material handling plants across Texas and beyond

What We Learned

From textiles to food and beverage to energy, SSI was always about integration — making equipment, automation, and people work as one. Evo carries that DNA forward: bridging legacy systems into modern architectures without losing the reliability that built our name.

Why It Matters

Legacy matters because it proves we’ve been trusted where it counts. Evo Engineering doesn’t just promise renewal — we’ve lived it, from shop floors to Fortune 1000 facilities. The same integrity that Michael Weedman built SSI with is the backbone of Evo today.
