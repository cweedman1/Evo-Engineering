Legacy Systems (SSI)

Roots · Integrity · Continuity

System Legacy

Evo Engineering stands on the foundation of Southwest Systems Integrators — a legacy built through decades of automation, fabrication, and industrial system integration.

Founded by Michael Weedman, SSI became synonymous with precision, reliability, and execution. From large-scale material handling systems to industrial automation projects across major facilities, the work consistently delivered under real-world conditions.

Clients Served

  • PepsiCo
  • Coca-Cola
  • Amazon
  • CenterPoint Energy (Houston)
  • Schlumberger
  • Tito’s Vodka — first automated bottling line installation
  • Artco-Bell Corporation (Temple, Texas)
  • Textiles, food & beverage, and industrial plants across Texas and beyond

What Carries Forward

SSI was always about integration — making equipment, automation, and operations function as a unified system. Evo carries that forward: modernizing legacy systems without sacrificing the reliability that built the name.

Why It Matters

Legacy matters because it reflects proven trust under real conditions. Evo Engineering doesn’t just pursue system renewal — it builds on a foundation that has already delivered at scale, across industries, and over decades.
