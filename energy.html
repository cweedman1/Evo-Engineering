Energy & Grid Renewal

Modernization · Resilience · Sovereignty

Grid Modernization Systems

Evo Engineering brings field-tested system design to grid modernization. From blackstart sequences to DER orchestration, we help operators coordinate assets, preserve context, and recover faster under stress.

What We Deliver

  • Grid Modernization - digital twins, analytics, and control systems that extend the life and reliability of existing infrastructure.
  • Renewable Integration - orchestration of wind, solar, storage, and conventional generation without losing operator visibility.
  • Resilience Engineering - outage simulation, restoration planning, and weatherization strategies that keep power flowing under stress.
  • Field-Proven Systems - solutions that bridge SCADA, IBR/DER assets, and operator workflows without disruption.

Why It Matters

Grid renewal is not only a capacity problem. It is a coordination problem across assets, controls, weather, operators, and capital. Evo engineers for stability, throughput, and systems that can be trusted when conditions are imperfect.
