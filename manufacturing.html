Manufacturing & Systems

Integration · Automation · Renewal

System Overview

Evo Engineering was built in the millwright world — real plants, real equipment, and real deadlines. We operate as general contractors and system integrators across food, beverage, and consumer goods manufacturing environments.

What We Deliver

  • Line Modernization — upgrading legacy production lines with new equipment and integrated PLC systems.
  • Material Handling & Sortation — conveyors, robotics, and sorting systems engineered for throughput and reliability.
  • Packaging & Palletizing — automated packaging lines and robotic palletizing systems that streamline operations end-to-end.
  • Full-Service Integration — design, installation, programming, and commissioning delivered as turnkey systems.

Why It Matters

Manufacturing is the engine of modern economies. Evo bridges legacy systems with modern automation — delivering higher throughput, safer operations, and extended equipment life without disrupting production. From Texas to Spain to anywhere production matters, Evo Engineering keeps industry moving.
