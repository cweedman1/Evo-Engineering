Manufacturing & Systems Integration

Evo Engineering grew out of the millwright world: real plants, real equipment, and real deadlines. We’ve been general contractors and system integrators for food, beverage, and consumer goods plants across the globe.

What We Deliver

  • Line Modernization — taking aging production lines, installing new equipment, and tying it all together with seamless PLC programming.
  • Material Handling & Sortation — conveyors, robotics, and sorting systems designed for throughput, reliability, and maintainability.
  • Packaging & Palletizing — automated packaging solutions and robotic palletizers that streamline operations from end to end.
  • Full-Service Integration — from design to installation to programming, Evo delivers turnkey systems that bring plants into the modern era.

Why It Matters

Manufacturing is the engine of economies. Evo’s strength is in bridging old and new — modernizing plants without disruption, delivering higher output, safer operations, and longer life for capital equipment. From Texas to Spain to anywhere production matters, Evo Engineering makes industry run stronger.
