Manufacturing & Systems

Integration · Automation · Renewal

Industrial Integration

Evo Engineering was built in the millwright world: real plants, real equipment, and real deadlines. That systems integration legacy carries into manufacturing work where mechanical systems, PLCs, material flow, and operators have to function as one system.

What We Deliver

  • Line Modernization - upgrading legacy production lines with new equipment and integrated PLC systems.
  • Material Handling & Sortation - conveyors, robotics, and sorting systems engineered for throughput and reliability.
  • Packaging & Palletizing - automated packaging lines and robotic palletizing systems that reduce handoffs and wasted motion.
  • Full-Service Integration - design, installation, programming, and commissioning delivered as maintainable operating systems.

Why It Matters

Manufacturing modernization fails when equipment, controls, and operators are treated as separate problems. Evo bridges legacy systems with modern automation to improve throughput, safety, and equipment life without disrupting production.
