About Us

Who We Are · What We Do · Where We’re Going

Who We Are

Evo Engineering is the continuation of a long-standing Texas-based automation and systems integration legacy. For decades, we have built complex ecosystems for Fortune 1000 clients including PepsiCo, Coca-Cola, Amazon, and Texas Instruments.

Evo’s DNA was forged in the most demanding environments — precision, accountability, and results delivered under strict regulatory and operational constraints.

What We Do

Evo Engineering is now evolving into its next phase: an international platform focused on energy, infrastructure, and technology renewal.

  • Evo Energy & Infrastructure — grid modernization, renewable projects, and advanced energy systems.
  • Evo Financial Services — asset-backed instruments, bond structuring, and private trust protocols.
  • Evo Manufacturing & Systems — advanced materials, fabrication, and automation systems.
  • Evo Software & Intelligence — secure platforms, AI development, and predictive system simulators.

Where We’re Going

Together, these divisions modernize legacy systems into resilient, sustainable platforms. Evo Engineering is positioned to lead the renewal of critical infrastructure across energy, finance, manufacturing, and intelligence — delivering long-term, sovereign solutions.
