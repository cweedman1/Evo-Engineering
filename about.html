About Evo Engineering

Legacy · Systems · Infrastructure

Who We Are

Evo Engineering continues a long-standing Texas-based automation and systems integration legacy. For decades, we have built complex operational ecosystems for Fortune 1000 clients including PepsiCo, Coca-Cola, Amazon, and Texas Instruments.

Our foundation was forged in demanding environments where precision, accountability, and execution under real-world constraints are non-negotiable.

What We Do

Evo Engineering is now evolving into a broader platform focused on infrastructure, energy systems, and advanced technologies.

  • Energy & Infrastructure — grid modernization, renewable systems, and large-scale energy integration.
  • Financial Systems — structured instruments, asset-backed models, and capital system design.
  • Manufacturing Systems — advanced materials, fabrication processes, and automation platforms.
  • Software & Intelligence — secure platforms, AI systems, and predictive modeling environments.

Where We’re Going

These domains converge into a unified objective: modernizing legacy systems into resilient, scalable infrastructure. Evo Engineering is positioned to support the renewal of critical systems across energy, finance, manufacturing, and computation.
